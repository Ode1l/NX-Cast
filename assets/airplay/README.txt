NX-Cast AirPlay storage
=======================

NX-Cast creates its device identity and trusted-client records in this
directory on first successful AirPlay startup.

Runtime files such as identity.bin and pairings.bin are private to this SD
card. Do not publish them, attach them to bug reports, or copy them into a
release package. Deleting them resets AirPlay identity and trusted pairings.

AirPlay support is experimental. URL/HLS playback and screen mirroring require
an iPhone and Switch on the same Wi-Fi. The build includes an auditable GPL
PlayFair compatibility backend, but real-device compatibility is not
guaranteed. Commercial FairPlay/DRM content, Apple certification, AWDL, and
AirPlay 2 multi-room audio are not supported.
